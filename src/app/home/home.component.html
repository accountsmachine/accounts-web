 Getting started So you want to file VAT returns...?
  • Before you can do anything you need to create a company configuration. We've made this easy, you punch in your company number, and most of the information is extracted from Companies House. For a VAT return, you don't need to complete the entire record. You do need to go to the Tax section and fill in your VAT VRN.
  • Next you need to link up accountsmachine.io with your HMRC VAT account. Go to the Status page, click on VAT next to your company. Clicking Initiate setup initiates the connection to HMRC.
  • On the Status page, you should be able to see your open VAT obligations at this point.
  • Next you need to go to the Books page and upload your accounting books. This should be a GnuCash file.
  • You are now ready to create a VAT return on the Filing page. Select Create VAT Filing, and step through the workflow, selecting your company and VAT return period.
  • When you are happy with your VAT return, you can go to the Filing page and select Manage Credits. On the Purchase tab, you have the option to buy VAT return credits with a payment card. Once you have credits, go back to the Filing screen where you can open your VAT filing and submit it.
