military_tech Status

The accountsmachine.io service is in an early Beta phase. The features you see are being trialled with a small community of early adopters prior to large-scale launch. Anybody can sign up, but the service is not being actively publicised, which makes it easier to work with the active community.

You can use accountsmachine.io to complete a VAT return up to the point of submission. HMRC are still processing the application for production VAT credentials.

Everyone is welcome to join the Discord group, and discuss, get help or give feedback.
