So you want to file VAT returns...?

navigate_next  Create a company configuration Before you can do anything you need to create a company configuration. We've made this easy, you punch in your company number, and most of the information is extracted from Companies House. For a VAT return, you don't need to complete the entire record. You do need to go to the Tax section and fill in your VAT VRN. navigate_next  Link HMRC VAT account Next you need to link up Accounts Machine with your HMRC VAT account. Go to the Status page, click on VAT in the table row for your company. Clicking Initiate setup initiates the connection to HMRC. You will be taken to the HMRC site and prompted to enter your Government Gateway ID, and then prompted to give Accounts Machine access to submit VAT returns for you. navigate_next  View VAT return status Once that is set up, you should be able to see the status of your VAT returns on the Status page. In particular you will be able to to see your VAT obligation history and see any return periods which are open. navigate_next  Upload accounting books Next you need to go to the Books page and upload your accounting books. This should be a GnuCash file. navigate_next  Create VAT filing You are now ready to create a VAT return on the Filing page. Select Create VAT Filing, and step through the workflow, selecting your company and VAT return period. navigate_next  Submit VAT filing When you are happy with your VAT return, you can go to the Filing page and select Manage Credits. On the Purchase tab, you have the option to buy VAT return credits with a payment card. Once you have credits, go back to the Filing screen where you can open your VAT filing and submit it.
